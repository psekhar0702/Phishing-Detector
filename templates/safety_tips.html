🛡️ Cyber Safety Tips

  • 🔐 Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
  • 📧 Be cautious of emails asking for personal information — avoid clicking unknown links or attachments.
  • 🌐 Always verify website URLs before entering sensitive information — check for HTTPS and padlock symbol.
  • 🦠 Keep antivirus, operating system, and browsers updated to patch security flaws.
  • 🚫 Never reuse the same password on multiple websites or share passwords via text/email.
  • 🧠 Educate yourself on common scams such as phishing, vishing, and smishing.
  • 🔒 Regularly back up important data to an external drive or encrypted cloud storage.
  • 📱 Lock devices with PINs, biometrics, and enable remote wipe in case of theft.
  • 🔍 Monitor your accounts and credit reports regularly for any unusual activity.
  • 🕵️‍♂️ Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial or confidential activities; use a VPN instead.
